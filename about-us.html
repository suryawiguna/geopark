FOREWORDS

On 20th September 2012, Batur Caldera has been officially included into network of UNESCO Global Geoparks Network as Batur UNESCO Global Geopark. This is a great honor, for that BUGG will become among the first and the pioneer for development of other global geoparks in Indonesia. The innovative approach by UNESCO on developing conservation area without abandoning sustainibility of geology, culture, as well environment, has given a beneficial impact for economic welfare, especially for the local community.

Geopark is a region that have an unique world class geology heritage. In its application, the concept of geopark has an intimate relationship with geology heritage, as well as biological and cultural diversity that must be well managed with involving local government and community. In the end it is for the collective interest of sustainable economic activity of local community through, geotourism, ecotourism, and culture tourism.

Besides as literature, this website can be used as guide for the readers and visitors that interested to visiting BUGG. VISION

The purpose behind the establishment of the UNESCO Global Geopark is to conduct and developing all of the tourism activity around special tourism region of Kintamani, also to provide an adequate or professional human resources in tourism.

MISSION

To create a special tourism area of Kintamani which fulfills the element of “sapta pesona”.

To provide an excellent services around the special tourism area of Kintamani.

To increase the satisfaction of every tourist who visits the special tourism area of Kintamani.

To increase the number of visits in special tourism area of Kintamani.

To increase regional income of the community who lived around Kintamani.
