why singamandawa?

Karanganyar Cluster consists of three villages in Kintamani , which is Kintamani, Sukawana, and Pinggan that located on western part of Batur Mountain Caldera. This Cluster has many attraction, started from the natural viewpoints on these three villages and famous cultural attraction on Dalem Balingkang Temple.

Here what you can found on Singamandawa Cluster!

(+) Kintamani villages has the most cutest and adorable race of dogs in Bali, called Kintamani dog. Here you can see many locals who breed this kind of animals.

(+) Wide area around this cluster grows many plantation such as orange, coffee, tomato, brings a strong natural fresh aura.

(+) With many plantation can be found around this cluster, you can taste the original crops fresh from the plant, especially the arabica coffee.

(+) visit the ancient temple of Dalem Balingkang to see its own history that might interest you, and many hinduism ceremony holds here every year.

(+) You can also visit the famous Penulisan temple. This temple offers a fair view that can be seen on the top of this area on Penulisan Temple, because its high ground location,

(+) Pinggan Village offer a stuning sunrise view upon its hills. Many tourist come here to see the most beautiful both sunrise and mountain view in the morning

When To Travel

Singamandawa Clusters looks more beautiful in clear days, each season has its own individual characteristic. This is what makes Singamandawa Clusters unique, varying contrasts of ecosystems that enable many plants to be viewed year round.

Seasons are generally divided into two, however like the other clusters there is a transition season. Green season, the lake waters are at their lowest and in some areas are replaced by a grass like green. These scenery are goods for photography.
