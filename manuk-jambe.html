why manuk jambe tarumenyan?

Manuk Jambe Tarumenyan Cluster consists of three villages in Kintamani that still holds the ancient Balinese culture, which is Kedisan, Buahan, and Trunyan that located on Eastern part of Batur Mountain Caldera. This Cluster has a strong spiritual aura and famous of its spiritual tourism, because how the local ways to burry their deceased relatives.

When To Travel

Located on eastern part of Batur Mountain Caldera, Manuk Jambe Tarumenyan doesnt have a different temperatures with the others clusters seasons are the same divided into rain and dry season. The clear skies in dry seasons that occurs on February - July, making sightings excellent in both the forest around and lakeview.

The two seasons are divided into a dry and rainy season, days are humid when rainfall occurs and temperatures are higher. The drier season, where daytime temperatures are cooler and days are hazy. During dry season many of the trees are in fruit, making this prime time for viewing and sighting the locals orchards.

Sometimes rainy season brings a spectacular show breezing mist, the condition of the mist accross the land makes perfect conditions for photography. And the perfect seasons to visit the trunyan graveyard are on the dry seasons because you needs to cross the lake to reach the graveyard.
